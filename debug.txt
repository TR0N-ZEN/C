how to start gdb
  https://sourceware.org/gdb/current/onlinedocs/gdb#Getting-In-and-Out-of-GDB


compile for debugging

  gcc -g -o ./${NAME}.debug.o ${NAME}.c

read furhter at
  https://sourceware.org/gdb/current/onlinedocs/gdb#Compiling-for-Debugging


run with debugger
  gdb ./${NAME}.debug.o

further information at
  https://sourceware.org/gdb/current/onlinedocs/gdb#Stopping-and-Continuing
  https://sourceware.org/gdb/current/onlinedocs/gdb#Breakpoints
